SAYBON — INVESTOR BUSINESS PLAN

Learn French. Feel French. Use French.

1. Executive Summary

SayBon is a Ghanaian-owned and built language learning platform designed to make French education accessible, engaging, and practically usable worldwide.

The platform integrates structured international-standard language learning, original music-based education, affordable DELF exam preparation, and professional French–English translation services into one scalable ecosystem.

SayBon is not simply a language-learning application. It is a complete language ecosystem connecting learning, certification, engagement, and real-world language use.

2. The Global Problem

  • High cost of quality French education
  • Over 80–90% learner dropout on traditional platforms
  • Fragmented and expensive DELF exam preparation
  • Weak transition from learning to real-world usage

Language education remains fragmented across multiple platforms, reducing learner confidence and long-term success.

3. The SayBon Solution

SayBon unifies language learning, certification, engagement, and application into one coherent experience.

Structured CEFR-Aligned Learning

  • Absolute Beginner
  • Beginner
  • Intermediate
  • Semi-Advanced
  • Advanced

Original Music-Based Education

SayBon produces 100% original educational music across gospel, pop, jazz, classical, hip hop, reggae, afrobeats, and Latin genres — accessed exclusively inside the SayBon platform.

Accessible DELF Exam Preparation

One-time payment per exam level with structured exam-aligned preparation, mock assessments, and guided strategies.

Professional Translation Services

French ↔ English translation services for individuals, institutions, NGOs, and corporate organizations.
